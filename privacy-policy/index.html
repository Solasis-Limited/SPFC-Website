Sex Positions for Couples

Privacy Policy

Sex Positions for Couples is operated by Solasis Limited. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it uses that information, and the choices available to users.

Last updated: 28 March 2026

Scope

This policy applies to the mobile app, backend services, and related support flows used by Sex Positions for Couples.

Information We Collect

  • Account and authentication information.
  • Onboarding preferences such as comfort level, experience level, and goals.
  • Saved positions, favorites, tried history, and ratings.
  • Partner-link state and sharing preferences.
  • Couple votes and shared wishlist selections.
  • Analytics consent choice.
  • Limited product analytics events if you opt in.
  • Assistant prompts and recommendation inputs needed to provide the feature.

How We Use Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Sync your saved preferences across devices.
  • Support couple linking and explicit sharing permissions.
  • Personalize position recommendations.
  • Provide educational assistant responses.
  • Maintain app security and detect errors.
  • Improve product quality when analytics consent is enabled.

Sharing and Partner Data

Partner-linked data is private by default and only shared according to the permissions you explicitly enable.

We may share selected preferences between linked partners only when the relevant sharing controls are turned on. You can update or remove partner linking from the app.

Analytics

The app includes a consent-based analytics system. Product analytics are off by default and are only sent when you enable analytics consent in onboarding or settings.

A limited set of account access events, such as sign-in request, sign-in completion, sign-in failure, and sign-out events, may be processed before analytics consent so that we can operate, secure, and improve the reliability of account access.

Analytics is intended to help us understand basic product usage and improve the app. We do not use analytics to expose intimate details publicly.

AI and Assistant Features

The assistant is educational and non-explicit. Assistant prompts may be processed server-side to generate safe guidance and recommendations.

We keep AI processing server-side and do not intentionally expose raw provider secrets to the mobile app.

Data Retention

We retain account and app data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

If you request deletion, we will handle the request through the support contact path provided in the app and remove or anonymize data where appropriate.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data, including authenticated access controls and server-side processing for sensitive features.

Your Choices

  • Sign out.
  • Disable analytics consent.
  • Unlink your partner.
  • Update sharing permissions.
  • Request account deletion.

Children

Sex Positions for Couples is intended only for adults of legal age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, use the support and data deletion form on this site.
